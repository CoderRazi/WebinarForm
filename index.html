Event Description

A webinar is an online event that allows people to attend a presentation, lecture, or workshop from anywhere in the world, as long as they have an internet connection. Webinars can be used for various purposes, such as training, education, marketing, and customer support. They typically involve one or more speakers who deliver a presentation, and attendees who can interact with the speakers through a chat or Q&A feature.
